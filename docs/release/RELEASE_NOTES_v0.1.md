## Bounded Claim

DRF + OMTIR is a governed action-accountability layer for tool-using AI
agents. Its frozen evidence demonstrates the mechanism in two bounded live
tool-using workflows and one bounded live model-driven proposal loop.

The Flight Recorder v0.1 package adds a local developer-facing product MVP:
init, demo, verify, receipt, and wrap. It is a product usability milestone,
not a new live-domain validation.
